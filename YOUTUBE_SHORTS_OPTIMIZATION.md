# YouTube Shorts Optimization Applied

## Research-Based Best Practices (2025)

### Key Changes Implemented:

#### 1. **Duration Optimization**
- Target: 30-45 seconds (research shows 15-60s performs best)
- Current: 10 segments × 3 seconds = 30 seconds
- Hook: First 2-3 seconds critical

#### 2. **Video-to-Image Ratio** ✅
- **Optimal**: 80% video, 20% images
- **Implementation**: 8 videos + 2 images = 10 segments
- **Pattern**: VVVV-I-VVVV-I (V=video, I=image)

#### 3. **Product Images**  
- **Issue**: Same image repeated
- **Fix Needed**: Download unique images from different products/angles
- **Implementation**: Get 5 different product images, use top 2

#### 4. **Subtitle Quality** 
- **Issue**: Overlapping, hard to read
- **Fix**: 
  - Short phrases (3-5 words)
  - 2-3 seconds per subtitle max
  - Larger font (32px for 1080x1920)
  - Proper positioning (120px from bottom)
  - Better contrast (white text, black outline)

#### 5. **Transitions**
- Smooth fades (0.2s) between segments
- No jarring cuts
- Optimized for mobile viewing

#### 6. **Hook Optimization**
- Attention-grabbing first 3 seconds
- Bright colors, bold text
- Pattern interrupt

#### 7. **Pacing**
- Scene change every 2-3 seconds
- Designed for rewatch/loop
- Fast-paced, engaging
